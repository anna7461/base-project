The Story Behind UX Misfit

“Here is to the crazy ones…” I am sure you know this famous phrase from the legendary Apple ad. It has a profound message inside. It was one of inspiration to start doing something that will help others make this world a better place, by creating delightful and useful things.

But a single person cannot do this alone… It would be hard even for a company. It is the whole army of people needed to do that.

And here is the idea…

  • Create a digital place to share UX stories presented from a different perspective.

  • Inspire and open designers mind for fresh ideas.

  • Save designers time with tools and resources, so they will be able to focus on what’s important.

I believe that this saved hours with a pinch of inspiration may lead you to remarkable results – improving someone’s life by creating a great product, service, or something different.

This is how I came up with creating UX Misfit blog & design resources.

Who am I?
