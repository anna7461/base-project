freecodecamp works!

freeCodeCamp is a donor-supported tax-exempt 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (United States Federal Tax Identification Number: 82- 0779546)

Our mission: to help people learn to code for free. We accomplish this by creating thousands of videos, and interactive coding lessons - all freely available to the public. We also have thousands of freeCodeCamp study groups around the word.

Donations to freeCodeCamp go toward our education initiatives, and help pay for servers, services, and staff.
